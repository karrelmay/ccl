BUWAN

juan karlos

\ bü-wän \ (n.) filipino
the earth's natural satellite that shines by the
sun's reflected light,revolves about the earth from west to east.



Buwan meaning Moon, talks about excessiveness in certain relationships and what happens when people break up. the song reflects on past moments when the singer was in love and how he misses this after losing it.
